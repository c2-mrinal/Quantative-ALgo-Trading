FAQ
1. Where is the data source from?

The data is sourced from TradingView which collects market information from various exchanges from all over the globe. We relay that information on our website.

2. Why is there no real-time stock data?

Exchanges want each website to pay fees for real-time data. This is why we use TradingView as our data source. As per their requirements we are only allowed to stream delayed data.

3. Why is there no data from certain exchanges?

We relay data bought and supplied by TradingView from many exchanges around the globe.

4. When a user clicks a symbol in a component, why is the site redirected to TradingView?

Since we do not own the data being relayed, in order to maintain credibility of the source we have added a source attribution to our components.

5. What is the time-range of data available?

All data in Forex and cryptocurrencyis real-time. Delayed data is marked D

6. What is the time range oftrading suggestions?

The technical analysis tool provides an easy to understand result without involving manual use of technical indicators. The time interval can be set from 1min to 1month
